I Live For Passion

I am your Arshi Khan or you can say as awaam ki jaan. I was born on 8th August, 1993 at the wonderful place called Kabul, Afghanistan. The two lovely creatures who brought me into this world, my dad Armaan Khan and my sweet mom Nadira Sultan. At the age of four they brought me to this awesome country called INDIA. It was at Bhopal I started my dreams, since schooling days I was keen interested in taking part in culturals and dance shows. After schooling I came out with flying colors, after graduating as an physiotherapist. Its in 2014 I began my dream career as an model in Mumbai.
